## Executive Summary
La Jolla Martial Arts is a Taekwondo-focused martial arts school in La Jolla serving children, teens, adults, and families. The business positions itself as more than a sports program: it emphasizes discipline, confidence, focus, respect, self-defense, fitness, and long-term personal development. The strongest differentiator on the current site is the reputation and teaching style of Master Sam, who is repeatedly described as experienced, disciplined, patient, individualized in his instruction, and especially effective with children.

The current website has useful raw messaging, but it also contains obvious template leftovers, inconsistent age ranges, duplicated content, and location/copy errors. For the rebuild, we should keep the strongest emotional and local messaging while removing all generic or conflicting text.

## Reusable Program Page Template
This is the recommended shared page structure for all program pages on the new site. It keeps the pages consistent for design and SEO while giving each program room to target its own audience and search intent.

## Shared Section Order
### 1. Hero
Use:
- Program name
- Age range or audience label
- One clear outcome-driven headline
- One short supporting paragraph
- Primary CTA: free trial / request info
- Secondary CTA: view schedule

### 2. Who It's For
Use a short section that says exactly who the program is designed for.

Include:
- age band or audience
- beginner-friendly note
- parent / student fit
- one sentence on what makes this program different

### 3. Challenges This Program Helps With
This section should translate objections into reasons to enroll.

Use:
- 3 to 6 bullets
- a short paragraph explaining why martial arts helps
- language pulled from real parent / student pain points

### 4. Key Outcomes / Skills
This is the section that replaces vague promises with concrete results.

Use:
- 4 to 8 bullets or cards
- examples: confidence, focus, respect, strength, discipline, self-defense, coordination

### 5. Stats / Proof / Why It Matters
Only include this section when the source material supports it well.

Good content types:
- verified stats
- short trust facts
- years of teaching experience
- long-term student outcomes
- review count / rating if verified

### 6. Benefits of La Jolla Martial Arts
This should be the main long-form body section on every program page.

Use:
- 4 to 6 sub-sections
- one short paragraph under each
- the strongest source material from the current program pages

### 7. What To Expect
Recommended new section for the rebuild.

Include:
- class atmosphere
- beginner experience
- coaching style
- safety / support
- progression or belt system if relevant

### 8. FAQ
Use 5 to 6 questions specific to that audience.

Best rule:
- answer real objections
- keep answers short and reassuring
- use FAQ schema on the final page

### 9. Testimonials
Use 2 to 4 testimonials that match the program audience.

Best practice:
- match kid reviews to kid pages
- match adult / kickboxing reviews to adult pages
- avoid reusing generic testimonials everywhere

### 10. Final CTA
Use one consistent closing block:
- free trial / request info
- schedule access
- location reminder
- short trust statement

## Shared SEO Rules For Every Program Page
- One primary keyword target per page
- Include `La Jolla` naturally in hero, body copy, and CTA
- One unique H1 only
- Custom title tag and meta description for each page
- Internal links to related programs, FAQ, schedule, and contact page
- At least one localized paragraph about training in La Jolla
- Avoid duplicate body copy between program pages
