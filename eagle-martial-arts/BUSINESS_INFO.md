# Eagle Martial Arts - Company Context & Services

## Company Overview
Eagle Martial Arts La Jolla is a family-owned martial arts school dedicated to instilling self-discipline, respect, and focus in students through structured, nurturing, and skillfully designed Taekwondo programs. Founded in 2006, the school serves La Jolla and the greater San Diego area, providing martial arts training for all ages from young children to adults.

## Target Audience
- Children ages 3-5 (Little Ninjas program)
- Kids and youth
- Teenagers
- Adults (ages 15+)
- Families looking for activities that build character and discipline
- Parents seeking confidence-building activities for their children
- Individuals interested in self-defense and physical fitness

## Services Offered

### Little Ninjas (Ages 3-5)
Classes designed specifically for kids 3-5 years of age, focusing on fundamental Taekwondo skills, discipline, and respect while having fun.

### Kids Martial Arts
Martial arts program for children that builds character, self-esteem, and teaches positive energy in a supportive environment.

### Teen's Martial Arts
Specialized programs recognizing teens' unique needs, providing an excellent outlet for physical activity while building discipline and focus.

### Adult Martial Arts
Classes designed for those aged 15 years and older, focusing on Taekwondo training, self-defense, and personal development.
