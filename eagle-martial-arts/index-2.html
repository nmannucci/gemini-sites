Meet The Founder

Master Sam Hadaf

With over 40 years of martial arts experience, Master Sam is a 6th Degree Certified Black Belt Master from the World Taekwondo Federation. He has proudly trained over 300 black belts around the world.

His philosophy extends far beyond physical techniques. He believes martial arts is a vehicle for building unshakeable character, discipline, and mental resilience that transforms every aspect of a student's life.
