Meet Your Instructor

Master Sam Hadaf

A devoted and compassionate instructor with 40+ years of teaching excellence

Master Sam has been teaching martial arts since 1983, earning his 6th Degree Black Belt certification from the World Taekwondo Federation. His articulate, focused, and dedicated teaching style—combined with an amazing sense of humor—makes learning martial arts both challenging and fun.

Using a scientific approach combined with perseverance, courtesy, and indomitable spirit, Master Sam has trained over 300 black belt students around the world. He regularly assesses students and visualizes the correct path for each individual, ensuring personalized growth for every martial artist who enters the dojo.
