# Summit Martial Arts - Business Info

## Company Overview
Summit Martial Arts is a premier Taekwondo and Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu academy located in Boulder, Colorado. We focus on building "Peak Performance" in both mind and body, combining traditional values with modern athletic training.

## Target Audience
- Children (Ages 4+) seeking discipline and confidence
- High-altitude athletes looking for cross-training
- Families interested in community-based personal growth
- Adults seeking practical self-defense and fitness

## Services Offered
- **Elite Taekwondo**: Traditional Olympic-style training for all ages.
- **Summit BJJ**: Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu for ground defense and technical mastery.
- **Youth Leadership Program**: Focused on character, school performance, and civic duty.
- **Adult Fitness Kickboxing**: High-intensity cardio and striking.
