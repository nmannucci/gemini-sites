About the Academy

BUILT ON
GRIT &
GREATNESS

"I've trained athletes who went on to play Division I, got signed to pro deals, and became coaches themselves. What they all had in common wasn't raw talent — it was the willingness to do the work when nobody was watching."

— Coach Marcus Webb, Founder & Head Coach

Atlas Baseball Academy was born in 2012 from a simple belief: great coaching changes lives. Founder Marcus Webb — a former AAA pitcher who spent nine seasons in professional baseball — returned to Phoenix with one mission: build the program he wished had existed when he was coming up.

From a single practice field and twelve players, Atlas has grown into the most respected youth baseball organization in the Southwest. Our athletes train year-round with the same discipline and intensity as the professionals, developing not just baseball IQ but the mental toughness that defines elite competitors.

ATLAS BASEBALL · PHOENIX AZ
Coach Webb, Atlas Field — 2023

Fourteen years and twelve state championships later, Atlas athletes are competing at every level of the game. Our 93% college placement rate speaks to what happens when genuine coaching meets genuine commitment. But our proudest statistic isn't a number — it's the character of the men our boys become.
