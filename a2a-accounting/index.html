Our Story

Built on Trust, Driven by Results

Founded in 2007, A&A Accounting was born from a simple belief: every individual and business deserves the same caliber of financial expertise that Fortune 500 companies enjoy. Our founder, Hrant Bedikian, brought years of corporate experience at Disney and Lionsgate to the streets of Downey — making elite financial services accessible to the community.

Today, we serve clients ranging from first-time tax filers to established corporations across the greater Los Angeles area. Our approach is personal: we take the time to understand your financial picture, identify every opportunity, and deliver results that make a tangible difference in your bottom line.

Our clients stay with us year after year because we treat their finances with the same care and precision we'd give our own. That's not just a philosophy — it's our promise.

Hrant Bedikian
Founder & Principal Accountant
