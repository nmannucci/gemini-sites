- [ ] All images: descriptive filenames (already good), alt text, WebP format, lazy loading
- [ ] Internal links to /coaches, /lessons, /memberships, /facilities pages
- [ ] Mobile-responsive (all sections)

## AI Search / AEO Optimization

- **Self-contained answer blocks** in the services and FAQ sections (extractable by LLMs without surrounding context)
- **Definition block** near top: "Better Baseball Training is a youth baseball academy in Rocklin and El Dorado Hills, CA offering private lessons, academy memberships, and travel teams for players ages 8-14U."
- **Avoid AI writing tells** — no "delve," "leverage," "comprehensive," em-dash overuse, or formulaic paragraph structures
- **Named experts with credentials** throughout (coach bios with alma maters and pro experience)
- **Specific numbers** over vague claims: "11 travel teams," "3,000 sq ft," "$250/month"

---

## Content Writing Guidelines

- Write like a coach talks — direct, confident, motivational
- Short sentences, high burstiness (vary sentence length)
- Lead with facts and specifics, not fluff
- Use "your player" and "your kid" — speak to parents (the buyers)
- No generic sports cliches — lean into BBT's actual differentiator: character development + pro-level coaching at the youth level
