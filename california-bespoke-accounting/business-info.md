## About / Mission

California Bespoke Accounting LLC offers bookkeeping, tax preparation, CFO, and other accounting services. They also help companies by providing advisory services, tax accounting, and all types of account-related services.

**Core belief:** Access to quality accounting, bookkeeping, and CFO services should not be limited to larger companies — small businesses deserve the same level of expertise typically reserved for big firms and large consulting engagements.

**Partnership philosophy:** "When you hire California Bespoke Accounting LLC., you're getting much more than an accounting firm. You're getting a true partner who will learn about your business, assess your needs, and provide the kind of counsel that will help you succeed even when you face stiff competition."

**Free consultation:** Offered to all new clients.

## Services

### 1. Bookkeeping & Accounting (Core Service)
Full-service bookkeeping and accounting for small businesses across a wide range of industries. Includes:
- Setting up sound financial systems
- Budgeting
- Cash flow management
- Preparation of financial statements
- Accounts payable & accounts receivable management

### 2. Tax Services
Strategic tax planning and compliance, including:
- Tax planning to minimize tax liability
- Preparation and **electronic filing** of federal, state, and local tax returns
- Ongoing tax accounting

### 3. QuickBooks Consulting Services
As **Certified Advanced QuickBooks ProAdvisors**, they offer:
- QuickBooks system setup and configuration
- Ongoing support and troubleshooting
- Training for business owners and staff

### 4. CFO Services (Outsourced / Fractional CFO)
Designed for growing businesses that need professional financial leadership without the cost of a full-time hire. Services include:
- Liaison with bankers, attorneys, vendors, and insurance agents
- Assistance obtaining bank financing and loan negotiations
- Cost/revenue benefit analysis & profitability analysis
- Software selection and implementation
- Financial forecasting and modeling
- In-depth financial analysis and reporting
- Custom-designed collection policies and procedures
- Budget preparation, reporting, and monitoring
- Analysis of equipment purchases and business expansions
- Establishing financial controls to reduce employee theft and increase profits
- Experience serving both **private and non-profit organizations**

### 5. Payroll Processing Services
Handled as part of the full-service accounting offering.

### 6. Advisory Services
General business planning and financial consulting.
