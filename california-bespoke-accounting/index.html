Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What types of businesses do you work with?
We work with a wide range of small and medium-sized businesses across diverse industries — retail shops, professional service firms, restaurants, contractors, healthcare providers — as well as 501(c)(3) nonprofit organizations. Whether you're a sole proprietor just starting out or an established business with 50+ employees, we tailor our services to fit your exact needs.
How is California Bespoke different from a typical bookkeeper?
Most bookkeepers simply record transactions and hand you a spreadsheet. At California Bespoke, we act as your financial partner — proactively identifying opportunities to reduce taxes, improve cash flow, and strengthen financial controls. Our team brings CFO-level thinking to every engagement, giving you strategic insights alongside accurate books. We also specialize in QuickBooks optimization, so your systems work as hard as your team does.
What does a free consultation include?
Your free consultation is a focused 30-minute conversation with our principal advisor — no sales pitch, no pressure. We'll review your current financial situation, understand your goals and pain points, and give you honest recommendations. If we're the right fit, we'll propose a custom engagement. If not, we'll point you in the right direction. Either way, you leave with valuable insights at no cost.
