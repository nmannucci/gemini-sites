About The Firm

Built to Help Small
Businesses Thrive.

Stash Bookkeeping was founded in May 2023 with a clear mission: take the burden of financial record-keeping off small business owners so they can focus on what they do best — growing their business.

Based in San Francisco and serving the entire Bay Area, our team of 13 professionals handles everything from monthly reconciliations and payroll processing to tax preparation and virtual CFO advisory. We match bank transactions across Stripe, Shopify, PayPal, and more — delivering accurate monthly reports you can rely on.

Whether you need ongoing bookkeeping, a one-time book cleanup, or strategic financial guidance, we provide professional, reliable service with extended hours and a personal touch.
