# Stash Bookkeeping - Company Context & Services

## Company Overview
Stash Bookkeeping is a bookkeeping firm based in San Francisco, CA, providing bookkeeping, payroll, and tax preparation services to small businesses in the Bay Area. They handle financial record-keeping so business owners can focus on growth, backed by accurate monthly reports and expert guidance. The company was founded on May 22, 2023.

## Target Audience
- Small businesses in the San Francisco Bay Area
- Business owners seeking outsourced bookkeeping and payroll
- Companies needing tax filing and compliance support
- Businesses of all sizes looking for financial reporting and advisory

## Services Offered

### Bookkeeping & Financial Records
- **Monthly Bookkeeping**: Systematic reconciliation matching bank transactions, credit cards, Stripe, Shopify, and PayPal entries
- **Book Cleanup**: Books cleaned fast — often within 30–60 days
- **Accounts Payable & Receivable Management**
- **General Ledger Maintenance**
- **Bank Reconciliations**
- **Financial Reporting**

### Payroll Processing
- **Federal and State Filings**
- **PTO Tracking**
- **Pay Runs**: Weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly
- **End-of-Year W-2s and 1099s**
- **Platforms**: ADP or QuickBooks Payroll

### Tax Preparation & Compliance
- **IRS and State Tax Return Preparation**
- **Quarterly Estimates**
- **Notice Support**
- **Audit Assistance**
- **Amended Returns**
- Business tax returns may be free for full-year bookkeeping clients

### Virtual CFO & Advisory
- **Monthly Expert Sessions**: Analyzing profits, margins, budgets, forecasts, valuations, and tax strategies
- **Customized Profit Reporting**

### Special Projects
- QuickBooks file cleanup
- Failed conversions and incorrect imports
- Integration issues from system migrations or accountant transitions
