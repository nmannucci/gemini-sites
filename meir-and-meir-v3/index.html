About The Firm

A Legacy of Trust &
Professional Excellence.

Meir and Meir, LLP has been a cornerstone of the Southern California accounting community since 1985. With roots that trace back to the late 1970s, our partners bring a wealth of experience that few firms can match. We take care of your business and tax matters so you can focus on what you do best — running your business.

Our founding partners, Saul and Maurice Meir, bring advanced degrees from Tel Aviv University, USC, and CSUN, combined with decades of senior auditing experience. Joined by partner Orit Cohen Gendelman, our firm offers a depth of expertise across audit, tax, and management consulting that is both comprehensive and deeply personal.

Efficiency, affordability, and of course, extreme discretion — these are the values that have guided our practice for over three decades.
