# BUSINESS - Company Context & Services

## Company Overview
Meir and Meir, LLP is an accounting firm based in Canoga Park and Beverly Hills, CA. They provide a comprehensive range of accounting, tax, and consulting services, with a focus on efficiency, affordability, and discretion. They pride themselves on handling business and tax matters so clients can focus on running their businesses.

## Target Audience
- Small to medium-sized businesses
- Individuals and families
- Non-Profit Organizations
- Business owners using QuickBooks
- Individuals with tax problems (IRS audits, back taxes, etc.)

## Services Offered

### Audit & Assurance
- **Audit of Employee Benefit Plans**: Efficient, affordable, and discreet audits for employee benefit plans.
- **Non-Profit Organizations**: Specialized audit and accounting services for non-profits.
- **Peer Review**: System and engagement peer reviews for CPA firms (Maurice Meir is a Team Review Captain).

### Business Services
- **CFO Services**: Part-time CFO services for businesses.
- **Forensic Accounting**: Investigating financial discrepancies and fraud.
- **Strategic Business Planning**: Planning for growth and stability.
- **Succession Planning**: Helping business owners plan for the future transition of their business.
- **New Business Formation**: Assistance with entity selection and setup.

### QuickBooks Services
- **QuickBooks Setup**: Proper installation and initial setup.
- **QuickBooks Training**: Training staff on how to use the software effectively.
- **QuickTune-up**: Reviewing and cleaning up QuickBooks files.
- **QuickAnswers**: Support for specific questions.
- **Buy QuickBooks and Save**: Reselling software.

### Tax Services
- **Tax Preparation**: For individuals and businesses.
- **Tax Planning**: Strategies to minimize liability.
- **Tax Problems**:
    - IRS Audit Representation
    - Non-Filed Tax Returns
    - Back Taxes Owed
    - Payroll Tax Problems
    - IRS Liens, Levies, and Wage Garnishment
    - Offer In Compromise
    - Bankruptcy
    - Innocent Spouse Relief

### Estate Planning
- Comprehensive estate planning services to manage and protect wealth.
