Frequently Asked Questions

Clear answers to common questions about our accounting, tax, and bookkeeping services.

What specific services do you offer?
We provide a comprehensive range of services including individual and business tax preparation (LLCs, S-Corps, C-Corps), monthly bookkeeping, payroll processing, sales tax filing, and strategic financial consulting/CFO services.
Do you work with clients outside of Pasadena?
Yes! While our office is in Pasadena, we serve clients throughout California and the United States. We utilize secure client portals and video conferencing to work seamlessly with remote clients.
What accounting software do you support?
We are certified ProAdvisors for QuickBooks Online and Xero. We can also assist with migrations from desktop software or other platforms like FreshBooks or Wave.
How much do your services cost?
Our pricing is tailored to the complexity of your needs. Simple tax returns start at competitive rates, while monthly bookkeeping packages are customized based on transaction volume. We offer transparent, flat-fee pricing for ongoing services so there are no surprises.
What is the difference between a Bookkeeper and a CPA?
A bookkeeper records daily financial transactions, while a CPA (Certified Public Accountant) analyzes that data to provide tax planning, strategic advice, and file tax returns. We provide both services under one roof for maximum efficiency.
How difficult is it to switch accountants?
It's easier than you think! We handle the entire transition process. We will contact your previous accountant to obtain necessary records (tax returns, depreciation schedules, etc.) so you don't have to manage the awkward conversation.
