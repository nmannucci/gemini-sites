Common questions

Everything you need to know about working with us on your website project.

What's included in a complete website build? +
Every project includes strategy and planning, custom design (no templates), responsive development, CMS integration, SEO foundations, performance optimization, security setup, analytics configuration, and a comprehensive handoff with training. We deliver a complete, launch-ready website.
Do you work with existing brand guidelines? +
Absolutely. We're experts at extending existing brand systems into digital experiences. If you don't have guidelines yet, we can create them as part of the project or recommend our brand identity service as a starting point.
What platforms do you develop on? +
We're platform-agnostic and recommend based on your needs. We work with WordPress, Webflow, Shopify, custom solutions with React/Next.js, and headless CMS setups. The right choice depends on your content needs, budget, and growth plans.
Will I be able to update the site myself? +
Yes! Every site we build comes with an intuitive content management system. We provide thorough training and documentation so your team can confidently update content, add pages, and manage the site independently.
What happens after launch? +
We offer ongoing support and maintenance packages to keep your site secure, updated, and performing optimally. Many clients also return for iterative improvements and new features as their business evolves.
How do you handle revisions? +
Our process includes structured revision rounds at each phase. We believe in collaborative refinement—your feedback shapes the final product. We work together until you're genuinely excited about what we've created.
