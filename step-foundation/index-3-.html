Who We Are

Where the court meets community and every kid has a champion.

STEP Foundation LA was born from a simple belief: every young person deserves someone in their corner. We use basketball as more than a game — it's a classroom where kids ages 5 to 17 learn teamwork, discipline, decision-making, and the confidence to chase their dreams.

Our mentors walk alongside youth through practices, community events, and real-life conversations. We create safe spaces where families, young people, and even law enforcement come together — breaking down walls, building trust, and proving that when a neighborhood works together, everybody wins.

"Graduation, not mere attendance."

— STEP Foundation motto

From the court to the classroom, our programs give young people the life skills and community connections they need to thrive — not just today, but for the long run.
